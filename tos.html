2. Types of cookies we use:

Data Retention & Deletion Policy

1. Data Retention: We retain customer data as long as the account remains active.

2. Account Termination: Upon account closure, data is securely deleted within 30 days unless legally required to retain.

3. Backups: Backups are maintained for up to 14 days and then permanently erased.

2. Abuse Complaints: We investigate abuse complaints including spam, hacking attempts, or illegal content.

3. Actions Taken:

  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent termination
  • Notification to relevant authorities (if required)

1. Overview: We maintain technical and organizational measures to protect data.

2. Measures:

2. Support Response:
